Tiger und Turtle

Duisburg, Deutschland

This time I only go alone. I started my trip on December 25. My Gf accompanied me to the airport. Then at 11:00 am my plane was board, and then we are takeoff. The trip from Jakarta to Frankfurt takes about 15 hours, with transit in Dubai for about 2-3 hours. After a long trip with a plane. Finally, I arrive in Frankfurt. My sister's picked me up at the airport, and after that, we get around the city of Frankfurt. My sister lives in Hannover, so we take another trip with ICE or fast trains; that trip takes about 5-6 hours from Frankfurt Haubtbahnhof to Hannover Haubtbahnhof. We arrived at Hannover Hbf at night, so we are so tired and sleepy. The next day we are not going anywhere because I want to enjoy the city from my sister's apartment. The next day I go to Eurocar to rental a car for these trips. I choose a Volkswagen GT, a compact car, but still can accommodate 5 people there because there's my sister's Bf and two friends. I don't want to go to mainstream places on this trip, so I go to rare places. So we take about 3 hours driving to Duisburg in Dortmund to go to this Tiger und Turtle. This place is a monument for people living around this district. From the photo, it's like a roller coaster, but it isn't. It's a path or stairs or something like that; we can go to the top of those just with our feet. We spend one and a half hours there. After that, we are going back to my sister's place.

~ December 2019
